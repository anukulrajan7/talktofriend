## What this is

TalkToFriend is a peer-to-peer video calling app licensed under the MIT License. The source code is public. You can run it yourself. You can look at every line of it.

This is not a company. There is no SLA. There is no support team. There is no enterprise tier.

## What we (don't) collect

- No accounts. No sign-up. No email.
- Your name is optional and unverified — type whatever you want.
- Room codes are ephemeral and auto-deleted after 24 hours.
- Chat messages are stored in SQLite and auto-deleted after 24 hours.
- Connection metadata (who's in a room) is held in memory only and never written to disk.
- No cookies. No analytics. No tracking pixels. No fingerprinting.

Full details in [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md).
